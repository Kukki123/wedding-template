Our Story

Kholi, a talented painter known for his vibrant canvases, met Anushka, a captivating writer with a penchant for whimsical stories, on a rainy afternoon in a cozy café. Kholi was sketching in his notebook, while Anushka scribbled in her journal. A clumsy waiter spilled coffee on Kholi’s sketchpad, and Anushka rushed to help. Their eyes locked in shared amusement as they cleaned up the mess. Conversation flowed effortlessly as they talked about their passions, dreams, and the little quirks of life. As days turned into weeks, their meetings became a cherished routine. Kholi’s paintings began to reflect the colors of Anushka’s stories, and Anushka’s writing was infused with the vibrancy of Kholi’s world. They explored the city together, finding beauty in its hidden corners. Their connection deepened, and with every shared sunrise, their love grew stronger, as if they were always meant to find each other.

Dec 11th, 2017, We Said Yes!

Kholi’s art and Anushka’s words combined beautifully, creating a love story where creativity and passion intertwined perfectly every day.
